Research Narrative

What ties my work together

My research builds end-to-end pipelines for industrial intelligence: from sensor signals (AE, vibration) and time–frequency analysis (STFT/CWT) to representation learning (CNNs, Transformers, ensembles) and decision-making that engineers can trust (explainability, uncertainty, and failure-consistent modeling for RUL).

Now (PhD direction)

Uncertainty-aware diagnosis and prognostics, explainable models aligned with physical failure modes, and robust generalization across machines and operating conditions.

Next

Physics-informed and multimodal RUL systems (AE + vibration + vision), domain adaptation for deployment, and open, reproducible benchmarks for condition monitoring.
